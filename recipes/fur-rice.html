Transforming Furikake Gohan

Description

An original take on the classical Furikake Gohan, the Yukihira style uses eggs coated by a golden coating using chilled chicken broth to create a golden jewel from the simplest ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Furikake
  • Nikogori
  • Chicken Wings
  • Bonito Stock
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Sugar
  • Light Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Broth
  • eggs
  • Scallioin
  • White Rice

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in frying pan and saut√© chicken wings until golden brown on both sides.
  2. Put evetything from frying pan into pot adding 1 Tbsp grated ginger, 700CC bonito broth, 1tbsp each of Sake, Mirin, Suagr, add 50cc of Light soy sauce.
  3. Pour broth into a container to cool. Once cooled, place in refrigerator to chill and harden. Debone chicken wings and cut into strips.
  4. Make the egg curds. crack eggs into a bowl add 1Tbsp of Sugar, and a pinch of salt.
  5. pour into frying pan on low heat, stirring rapidly to create curds. once done move to plate
  6. one broth hardens, remove from container and cut aspic into 1cm x 1cm squares.
  7. pour aspic,eggs curds,and chicken strips over hot rice. sprinkle with diced spring onion and enjoy!
